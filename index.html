Welcome to the Robert Gordon University (RGU) Virtual Campus Information Pages



The RGU: Virtual Campus delivers a state-of-the-art programme of undergraduate and postgraduate courses to a community of learners from around the globe. The Campus is used not only to deliver online courses to distance learning students, but also to provide support materials to our students attending campus.

Have a look around.

This site predominantly provides information on our online distance learning courses and how the Virtual Campus can help support your learning. If you are interested in coming to RGU to study full or part-time please also visit the RGU Website at http://www.rgu.ac.uk for fuller information about our traditionally taught courses. You can read about our most recent developments by clicking the News Items link on the left-hand side.
